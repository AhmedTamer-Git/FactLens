✦ Our Story

Fighting Misinformation

FactLens was built on one belief: everyone deserves access to accurate, verifiable information. We harness AI to make truth-checking instant, transparent, and accessible.

Our Mission

In an era of rampant misinformation, FactLens provides a trusted, AI-powered platform that cross-references claims against thousands of credible sources — returning a clear, confidence-scored verdict within seconds.

We believe that informed citizens build stronger societies. Our mission is to lower the barrier to fact-checking so that anyone, anywhere can verify what they read before they share it.

How FactLens Works

From raw text to verified verdict in four simple steps

1
📋

Paste the Claim

Copy any news headline, article excerpt, or social media claim and paste it into FactLens.

2
🤖

AI Analysis

Our model breaks down the claim, identifies key assertions, and searches relevant sources.

3
🔍

Source Cross-Check

The AI cross-references against a curated database of trusted news outlets and fact-checkers.

4
📊

Credibility Verdict

Receive a scored verdict — Verified True, Misleading, False, or Unverifiable — with full explanation.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make

🎯

Accuracy First

We obsess over accuracy. Every verdict is backed by evidence, and we continuously improve our model to reduce false positives.

🔓

Transparency

We always show our work. You can see the exact sources cited, the explanation behind the verdict, and the confidence level.

⚖️

Political Neutrality

FactLens has no political agenda. We evaluate claims on factual merit alone, regardless of who made them or what they support.

🌍

Accessibility

Fact-checking should be available to everyone. We support Arabic and English content with plans to expand further.

🔒

Privacy

Your queries are yours. We do not sell your data, track your reading habits, or share your history with third parties.

🚀

Continuous Improvement

We treat every piece of user feedback as an opportunity to learn, refine, and deliver better verdicts over time.
